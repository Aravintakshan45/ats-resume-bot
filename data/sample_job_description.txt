Data Analyst Job Description

We are seeking a skilled Data Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for analyzing complex datasets, creating visualizations, and providing actionable insights to support business decisions.

Key Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets using SQL and Python
- Create data visualizations using Tableau or Power BI
- Develop and maintain dashboards and reports
- Perform statistical analysis and predictive modeling
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify business opportunities
- Present findings to stakeholders and management
- Ensure data quality and integrity
- Automate data collection and analysis processes

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
- 2-4 years of experience in data analysis
- Strong proficiency in SQL
- Experience with Python or R for data analysis
- Proficiency in data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI)
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Experience with Excel and data manipulation

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree in related field
- Experience with machine learning algorithms
- Knowledge of data warehousing concepts
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure)
- Understanding of A/B testing and experimentation
- Experience with ETL processes
